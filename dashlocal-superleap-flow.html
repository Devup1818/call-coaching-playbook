⚠ The Gap

DashLocal captures intent (views, calls, direction clicks) but does not push leads into Superleap automatically. You can't track which Google searches → which admissions. The full funnel ROI is invisible.

✅ The Fix

Tag DashLocal-origin leads in Superleap. Auto-push inbound calls & product enquiries from DashLocal into the Superleap opportunity pipeline. One unified view from discovery → admission.

📈 Current Issues in Superleap

From the live opportunity dashboard — Sector 18

Speed-to-lead is erratic — First call ranges from 0 to 414 minutes. Industry best is under 5 min. → Auto-trigger WhatsApp/SMS within 1 min
High drop-off at "Ringing" — Most leads die at "Not Answering." No retry cadence. → 3-call + WhatsApp + SMS sequence over 24 hrs
Only 2 lead sources — Meta Form + Website. Google Ads, Instagram DM, YouTube missing. → Add all source tracking
Location Issue = #1 rejection — People outside Noida enquire but can't travel. → Route to nearest center or offer online
Single owner bottleneck — All leads assigned to "Sonia." No distribution. → Auto-distribute by center or round-robin
No conversion tracking — No lead shown in "Admitted" stage. Funnel ends at "Follow-up." → Define clear stages: Enquiry → Visit Scheduled → Visit Done → Admitted
