# Counselor Playbook — Lakmé Academy (Sector 18)
### Call Script, Objection Handling & Daily Drill

> **Purpose**: Every call's goal is a center visit. Not full counseling. Not fee discussion. Just the visit.

---

## 1. THE CALL SCRIPT

### Opening (15 sec)
> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Lakmé Academy. You enquired about our courses — which one are you interested in? Makeup, hair, skin, or cosmetology?"

**Rule**: Ask a specific question immediately. Don't say "how can I help you?"

### Qualify (30 sec)
| Question | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| "Have you done 10th or 12th?" | Check eligibility |
| "When do you plan to start?" | Urgency |
| "Which area do you stay in?" | Location fit |
| "Is this for a career or a side skill?" | Motivation |

### Pitch (30 sec)
> "We have foundation and advanced courses starting from [X] months. Our students get placed at Lakmé Salons, Nykaa, and 50+ other brands. Since you're in Noida, Sector 18 is our main center — fully equipped studio, Lakmé-certified trainers."

**Don't**: List all courses. Match one course to their need.

### Objection → Redirect (20 sec)
See Section 2 below.

### Close for Visit (15 sec)
> "Why don't you come see the center? Takes 10 minutes. I'll show you the studio and you can meet a trainer. Today at 4 or tomorrow morning?"

**Rule**: Offer exactly 2 time slots. Not "whenever works for you."

### Confirmation (10 sec)
> "Great, I'll send you the address on WhatsApp. See you at 4."

---

## 2. OBJECTION HANDLING (Top 5)

| Objection | Say This | Then |
|-----------|----------|------|
| **"Fees too high"** | "I understand. Most of our courses start from just ₹[X] and we have EMI options. Our graduates earn ₹15-25K starting salary. That's 3-4 months to recover the investment." | Share placement sheet on WhatsApp |
| **"Will think about it"** | "Totally fair. What's the one thing you're unsure about — the course, the time, or the fees?" | Isolate the real objection |
| **"Location is far"** | "We're right next to [landmark]. 5 min walk from Sector 18 metro. Let me send you the exact location." | Pin drop on WhatsApp |
| **"Comparing with VLCC/Orane"** | "We're the only one backed by Lakmé + HUL. Our curriculum is designed by Lakmé's creative directors. And we have direct placement into Lakmé Salons." | Share Google review screenshot |
| **"Need to ask parents"** | "Of course. Bring them along when you visit. I can explain everything to them too. Tomorrow at 4?" | Convert to visit with parents |

## 4. CALL ATTEMPT PROTOCOL

| Attempt | When | How |
|---------|------|-----|
| 1st | Within 2 min of lead | Call + WhatsApp brochure |
| 2nd | 30 min later | Call (different time slot) |
| 3rd | Next morning 10 AM | Call |
| 4th | Next evening | WhatsApp voice note: "Just checking if you had any questions about the course." |
| 5th | Day 3 | SMS with link + "Last call — free demo available" |

**If no response after 5 attempts**: Move to weekly broadcast list. Do not delete.

---

## 5. DAILY DRILL (15 min huddle)

Every morning, counselor reviews:
1. **Yesterday's calls**: How many → connected → interested → visit booked → visit done → admitted
2. **Today's follow-ups**: Who needs a callback, who has a visit scheduled
3. **Missed calls from last 24 hrs**: Call them back first

**Track these 3 numbers only:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| % leads contacted within 5 min | >80% |
| % calls → visit booked | >30% |
| % visits → admission | >50% |

---

## 6. WHAT NOT TO DO

- ❌ Don't read the entire course list — ask first, then match
- ❌ Don't discuss fees before establishing value
- ❌ Don't say "I'll call you later" without setting a time
- ❌ Don't let the call go beyond 7 minutes
- ❌ Don't argue with objections — acknowledge, then redirect

---

## 7. CUSTOMER TYPES & APPROACH

| Type | Profile | Approach |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Fresh 12th pass** | Looking for career, parents involved | Emphasize placement, Lakmé brand, invite parents to visit |
| **Homemaker** | Upskill, flexible hours | Highlight part-time/weekend batches, EMI options |
| **Working professional** | Career switch, weekend | Show fast-track courses, salary potential |
| **Comparison shopper** | Called VLCC/Orane first | Lakmé brand + placement = differentiator |
| **Budget conscious** | Low upfront capacity | EMI, pay-after-placement option, ROI calculation |
